Applied Machine Learning Scientist

Research areas and applied systems

Adaptive Reinforcement Learning

I develop reinforcement learning agents for human–AI collaboration, focusing on hierarchical skill learning, partner-aware adaptation, and decision-making in multi-agent environments (e.g., Overcooked).

Federated & Continual Learning

I work on learning under distribution shift in decentralized settings, developing methods such as prototype-based continual learning, generative modeling (GANs/VAEs), and advanced aggregation strategies (including second-order methods) for federated systems.

Real-Time Physiological AI

I build systems that process EEG/ECG and multimodal streams in real time, enabling AI agents to adapt to human cognitive and emotional state while providing interpretable behavior.
